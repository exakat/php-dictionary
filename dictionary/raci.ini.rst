RACI Model
----------

RACI model is an accountability model, which list 

+ Responsible: who complete the task.
+ Accountable: who validate the correct completion of the deliverable
+ Consulted: who provide expert advice on the situation
+ Informed: who is kept informed, though do not participate directly

Other responsability models exist.
