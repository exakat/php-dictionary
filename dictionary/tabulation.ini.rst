Tabulation
----------

A tabulation is a whitespace character, that often represents the equivalent of four spaces characeters.

A tabulation is represented by the escape sequence ``\t``, in double quote strings.

.. code-block:: php
   
   <?php
   
   echo \t;
   
   ?>
