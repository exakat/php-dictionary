.. _and:

And Operator
------------

``and`` is the logical and operator. It is synonym with the symbol and operator ``&&``, though it has a different precedence level. 

``and`` is a PHP keyword.

.. code-block:: php
   
   <?php
   
   $a = 1 & 2;
   
   ?>
