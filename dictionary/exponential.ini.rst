Exponential
-----------

The exponential is a mathematical function. It satisfies the equalities : `exp($a + $a) = exp($a) * exp($b)`, and `exp(0) === 1`.

.. code-block:: php
   
   <?php
   
   $e = exp(1); // also known as M_E
   
   ?>


`Documentation <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Exponential_function>`__

Added in PHP 8.1
