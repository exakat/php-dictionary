Tarpit
------

A tar pit is a deliberate slowdown of abusive or unwanted requests to waste an attacker’s time/resources. It is related to the honeypot, but also slightly different.

`Documentation <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tarpit_(networking)>`__
